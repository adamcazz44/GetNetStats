- **Speed-test videos:** `internet speed test`, `how to test internet speed`, `download upload speed`, `ping and jitter explained`, `is my internet slow`, `broadband speed test`
- **IP / location videos:** `what is my IP address`, `check my IP`, `IP lookup`, `does my IP reveal my location`, `public vs private IP`, `ISP lookup`
